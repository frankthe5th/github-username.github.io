About Us

MINISO, began in September 2013 through the philosophy of “simplicity, nature and good quality” and the brand proposition of “returning to nature”. The Japan and China based designer retailer was co-founded by Japanese designer Mr. Miyake Junya and Chinese young entrepreneur Mr. Ye Guofu in Tokyo, Japan, with the former serving as chief designer. The MINISO brand now operates more than 4,000 stores in 78 countries and regions, with expansion in 2017 to the US as a major market focus through a greater demand for more emphasis on life supplies with simple and modern aesthetics. With the core values of designed-forward products, affordable price points, popular brand collaborations, and comfortable shopping experience, MINISO gains popularity among consumers and sets consumption trend in the household and consumer goods market. Taking product management and cost performance as its priority, MINISO holds a high standard on selecting designers, suppliers, and manufacturers all over the world. More than 80% of the design implementation hails from Japan, Korea, Sweden, Denmark, Singapore, Malaysia, China, and so on. And by working directly with top-tier suppliers and manufacturers, MINISO strives to control the pricing strictly to benefit the consumers. While pursuing innovative and economical products, collaborations with Marvel, We Bare Bear, Adventure Times and other brands have been developed by MINISO in order to offer trendy licensed products with low costs to broader customers. MINISO attaches great importance to consumer shopping experience and devotes itself to creating a culture of quality service, to convey beliefs such as health, leisure, quality, and creativity.
